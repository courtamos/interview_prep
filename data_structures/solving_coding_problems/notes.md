## What Are Companies Looking For?

Companies aren't just looking at your ability to solve a difficult problem, they're looking for the following:

1. Analytic Skills

   - How can you think through a problem and analyze things, the process you take to understand a problem and solve it.

2. Coding Skills

   - Is your code clean, well organized, and readable.

3. Technical Skills

   - Do you know the fundamentals of programming

4. Communication Skills

   - Do you match the culture fit of the company and are you able to comunicate effectively while working though challenging problems
